Hunza Valley

Hunza is a mountainous valley in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan. Hunza is situated at the bank of Hunza river in the northern part of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan.

Naran, Kaghan Valley

The best time to visit Naran is in the summer months due to less probability of snow fall and blocked roads but the city is accessible in the summer months as well.

Golaghmuli Valley

Golaghmuli Valley or Koh-i-Ghizer or Valley of Martyrs or Shandur Valley is a high mountainous valley in the Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan.

Gilgit Valley

Gilgit Valley is a valley located in Gilgit District in Gilgit-Baltistan, northern Pakistan. The Gilgit River flows through the valley.

Kunar Valley

Kunar Valley is a valley in Afghanistan and Pakistan. In Afghanistan the length of the valley is almost entirely narrow with steep and rugged mountains on both sides.

Miranzai Valley

The Miranzai Valley, also Hangu, is a mountainous valley situated in Pakistan. It is made up of two valleys, draining from the southwest into the Kurram and northeast into the Kohat Tai.

Gojal Valley

Gojal, also called Upper Hunza, is situated in the far north of Pakistan. In 2019, Gojal Valley became the second Sub-Division within the Hunza District.

Kalam Valley

Kalam is a valley located at distance of 99 kilometres from Mingora in the northern upper reaches of Swat valley along the bank of Swat River in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan.

Fairy Meadows Pakistan

Fairy Meadows, named by German climbers and locally known as Joot, is a grassland near one of the base camp sites of the Nanga Parbat, located in Diamer District, Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan.
